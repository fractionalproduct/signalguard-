# Notifications

Notifications tell the owner when something needs attention, while keeping critical safety alerts visible.

## Key requirements

- **MVP channels:** in-app notifications and email through the selected email provider.
- **Push and SMS are later possibilities, not MVP requirements.**
- **Critical alerts are always enabled in-app.**
- **Emergency Stop sends a critical notification.**
- **Notifications must not hide paper-trading status.** The product must show that no real money is being used.
- **Briefings are scheduled.** Morning, intraday, evening, daily, weekly, and monthly summaries are generated by cloud services, not by a laptop process.

## Notification types

1. **Critical safety alerts** — Emergency Stop, broker/data failures, risk blocks, and order-reconciliation issues.
2. **Action-required alerts** — proposals needing owner review, MFA-required actions, expired approvals, or setup gates.
3. **Briefings and summaries** — morning, intraday, evening, daily, weekly, and monthly updates.
4. **System health notices** — worker, scheduler, provider, or data-feed issues.
5. **Performance notices** — realized/unrealized P&L and benchmark reporting summaries.

## Plain-language rule

Important safety notices must be hard to miss, but no notification should imply guaranteed profit or real-money trading.
